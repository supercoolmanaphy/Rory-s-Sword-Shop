Use Cases

Our handcrafted bronze swords are designed to serve multiple real-world purposes:

  • Renaissance Faires Eye-catching swords perfect for vendors and performers at Ren faires across the country.
  • Historical Reenactment Authentically cast blades that meet the needs of serious reenactors seeking period-accurate weapons.
  • Display & Collecting Beautiful show pieces for collectors, museums, or home décor for history lovers.

Our Clients

We serve two main types of customers:

  • History Enthusiasts Individuals passionate about history who want an authentic, hand-made piece to own or display.
  • B2B Wholesale We offer bulk pricing and act as a manufacturer for retailers and specialty shops. Become a reseller and stock our swords in your store.

Target Demographic

Our primary market is made up of:

  • Historical Reenactors Members of living history groups, medieval combat societies, and reenactment clubs who value accuracy and craftsmanship.
  • History Enthusiasts Hobbyists, collectors, and learners fascinated by ancient civilisations and their weapons and tools.

Advertising Strategy

We reach our audience through targeted, community-focused outreach:

  • Facebook Group Ads Targeted advertisements placed directly within active historical reenactment and living history Facebook groups, where our core audience already gathers.
  • Social Media Posts Regular posts showcasing the casting process, finished swords, and behind-the-scenes content to build brand awareness and engagement.
  • Word of Mouth Attending Ren faires and reenactment events in person to demonstrate quality and build lasting customer relationships.
