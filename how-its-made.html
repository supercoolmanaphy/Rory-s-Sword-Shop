How It's Made

1. Filling the Crucible

We weigh out Copper and Tin to make the Bronze mixture. We used 10% Tin and 90% Copper in this mix.

2. Add Borax as Flux

In order to pull contaminants out of the metal, Borax is added. Impurities bond to the Borax, and our molten metal becomes cleaner.

3. Pouring the Metal

Carefully, the liquid Bronze is poured into my custom made Sword mold.

4. Sword in the Mold

This is the sword as it comes out of the mold. It will need to be trimmed down, polished and sharpened.

5. Finished Swords

Here are some finished swords we made. Any imperfect swords can be made into spearheads, or melted down.
